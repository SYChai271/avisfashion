About Us

Who we are

Avis Fashion is a fashion brand that is based in Singapore. We are a group of students who are passionate about fashion. We believe that fashion is a way to express yourself and express yourself in a way that is not only fashionable but also meaningful. Through the use of technology, we aim to create a platform for people to express themselves through fashion. Moreover, by using this platform, we can put an end to Fast Fashion. Ultimately, our goal is to create a more sustainable fashion industry.
